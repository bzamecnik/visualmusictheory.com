The story behind

Hi, I'm Bohumír, a software engineer and a music lover. For 10+ years I'm singing in a choir, playing guitar and other instruments and I've been always wondering how stuff works, even in music.

Alhough I attended classic music lessons for a few many years I've found my way to understand the harmony and acoustic via maths and visualization and eventually I've been creating software to visualize tones in live music.

Also I've always found the way how music theory is presented hides the surprisingly simple principles under a thick cover of tradition that evolved for centuries.

This unfortunately frightens many people who just want to share their emotions via music and not to memorize strange things. Although some are talented with great hearing almost everybody trains their eyes all the time to absorb a huge amount of visual information.

So I think it's time to turn music into visual images and be able to see the patterns of what happens in music.

Now I've decided to create lessons aimed to teach you the important principles of music that can be used in practice for the rest of life - without the clutter and with the help of visualization.

Currently the lessons are being born. And you have a unique opportunity to get a personal skype lesson for FREE.

You're free to ask your own questions and able to shape how the lessons will look like. This offer is limited - you can subscribe and you win a lesson if you're email will be chosen randomly.

Still you have a decent chance of getting the lesson. What if you aren't lucky to win? Feel free to write me and we can discuss.

Thank you and I'm looking to hear from you soon. Good luck!
